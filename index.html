Machine Learning Ilorin

New A research-first AI/ML community based in Ilorin, focused on Nigerian contexts.

Machine Learning Ilorin is a community for people who want to do the work: meet, research, experiment, train, evaluate, and document.

Ilorin is our base. Nigeria is our primary context. We are interested in open-source models, local datasets, model evaluation, and practical AI/ML experiments that make sense for the realities around us.

What this is

A small group with a serious research habit.

MLI is not an AI hype group, a passive event series, or a certificate circle.

It is a place to read carefully, reproduce ideas, run experiments, compare results, and leave behind useful notes, prompts, datasets, notebooks, reports, or demos.

How we work
  • We study papers, model reports, and technical guides before turning them into small experiments.
  • We work with open-source models and evaluate where they succeed or fail in Nigerian contexts.
  • We care about Nigerian English, Nigerian languages, education, commerce, public information, health, agriculture, and everyday use cases.
  • We welcome beginners, but the culture rewards active contribution over passive interest.
